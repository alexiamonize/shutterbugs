Good questions

Things parents usually ask.

Does my child need their own camera?
No. We provide cameras for every in-studio class — durable point-and-shoots for younger kids, mirrorless cameras for teens. If your child has a camera (or even a phone) they love, they're welcome to use it.
What if my kid has never touched a camera?
Perfect — most of our students start there. Week one is designed for total beginners, and small groups mean nobody gets left behind.
Are classes in-person or online?
Both. In-studio classes run at our space, and online cohorts follow the same weekly missions using a phone or any camera at home, with live video sessions and instructor feedback.
How do you handle photos of the kids?
We never publish a child's photo or name without written parent consent. Class galleries are private, shared only with enrolled families through a password-protected link.
What happens if we miss a week?
Every mission is recorded and posted to your family dashboard, and kids can join a make-up session in a parallel cohort at no extra cost.
